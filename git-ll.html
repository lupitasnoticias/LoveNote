Dear Git,
What is it that you want from our relationship? I hardly understand what it is that you do.

Thanks for being speedy! (FYI: Sometimes people need to learn to take their time learning!)

I have to admit I do admire your integrity. But how well can you really keep me safe? With the cloud revealing all my weaknesses, TSA constantly stalking me, and Edward Snowden seeking Asylum in Russia I don't know who is left to defend our integrity. I hope you're on our side.

Although if I were to choose your single best quality, I'd say it's your unwavering support in all aspects of my sights. Not only do you follow me wherever I go, but you actually understand who I am. When it comes to romance, spontaneity can by key as long as the other follows. Babe, you got that down pact!

We're never breaking up! I may never look back, so thanks for always being there for me Git <3
